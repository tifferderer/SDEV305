Services

We design custom light strands that perfectly contour the lines of your house. Our team will install the lights for your holiday season or special occasion and return after the holiday season or event to remove the lights for you. The custom light strands are yours to keep.

Lighting Package

Your lighting package includes light strands custom designed for your home, gutter clips that keep the light bulbs in place and a timer to turn the lights on and off for you.

The custom light strands, clips and timers are all yours to keep.

Installation and Removal

We install your lighting package and take them down for you to use for years to come. We work with you to get your lights down in a timely manner, we know there are HOA rules and regulations require lights to be down by a certain time, and we will schedule accordingly.

Once you have purchased your light strands we will reinstall them for you the next holiday season or event for only our installation fee.

Storage and Warranty

We warranty our installation and equipment for the season. If a light burns out and/or falls down we come and replace it at no cost to you.

When you store your lights with us and have us install your lights the next season we warranty all the bulbs and clips for 5 years from the first installation date.
